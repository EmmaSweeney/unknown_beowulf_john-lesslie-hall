List of Words and Phrases Not in General Use

Atheling

Prince, nobleman.

Bairn

Son, child.

Barrow

Mound, rounded hill, funeral-mound.

Battle-sark

Armor.

Beaker

Cup, drinking-vessel.

Begear

Prepare.

Bight

Bay, sea.

Bill

Sword.

Boss

Ornamental projection.

Bracteate

A round ornament on a necklace.

Brand

Sword.

Bernie

Armor.

Carle

Man, hero.

Earl

Nobleman, any brave man.

Eke

Also.

Erst

Formerly.

Erst-worthy

Worthy for a long time past.

Fain

Glad.

Fey

Fated, doomed.

Float

Vessel, ship.

Foin

To lunge (Shaks.).

Glory of Kings

God.

Grewsome

Cruel, fierce.

Helm

Helmet, protector.

Henchmen

Retainer, vassal.

Hight

Am (was) named.

Holm

Ocean, curved surface of the sea.

Lief

Dear, valued.

Mere

Sea; in compounds, “mere-ways,” “mere-currents,” etc.

Mickle

Much.

Nathless

Nevertheless.

Naze

Edge (nose).

Ness

Edge.

Nicker

Sea beast.

Quit, quite

Requite.

Reave

Bereave, deprive.

Settle

Seat, bench.

Swinge

Stroke, blow.

Targe, target

Shield.

Uncanny

Ill-featured, grizzly.

Unnethe

Difficult.

War-speed

Success in war.

Web

Tapestry (that which is “woven”).

Weeded

Clad (cf. widow’s weeds).

Ween

Suppose, imagine.

Weird

Fate, Providence.

Whilom

At times, formerly, often.

Wielder

Ruler. Often used of God; also in compounds, as “Wielder of Glory,” “Wielder of Worship.”

Wight

Creature.

Wold

Plane, extended surface.

Wot

Knows.

Younker

Youth.
